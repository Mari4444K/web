Traditional food

This page will show you some traditional German food. They are all very delicious, so please try them.

Wurst

“Wurst” means sausage in German. There are many different types of sausages, such as the long, thin Thüringer wurst, the short Nürnberger wurst, and the Münchner wurst. Wurst is a very popular food in Germany, and daily conversation using the word "Wurst″ is often seen. For example, "Das ist mir Wurst" translates literally to " It' s a sausage for me". However, it is used in the meaning of "I don't care".

Brezel

‟Brezel” means pretzel in German. It is a traditional German sweet bread. It is characterized by its unique knotted shape. There are many theories about the reason why it is shaped like this. For example, some traditions say it is shaped like a monk praying. According to another tradition, the three holes symbolize the Christian Trinity. In the city of Speyer, Germany, the "Brezel Fest" is held every year in July.
